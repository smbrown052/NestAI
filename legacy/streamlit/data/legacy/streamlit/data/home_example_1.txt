Spacious, Sunlit Top-Floor Condo in Parkfairfax - Treehouse Views!
Enjoy private, lush green views from every window in this beautifully updated, 805 sq. ft. 1-bedroom, 1-bathroom upper-level end unit. Tucked away with a serene "treehouse feel," this condo combines quiet privacy with unbeatable convenience and premium community amenities.

Property Highlights:
Bright & Airy: End-unit positioning offers an abundance of natural light.
Move-In Ready: Freshly painted throughout with gorgeous hardwood floors.
Modern Conveniences: Updated kitchen and in-unit washer/dryer.
Pet-Friendly: Considered on a case-by-case basis (under 85 lbs.).

All-Inclusive Living (What's Included in Rent!)
Your rent covers:
Utilities & Services: Gas, water, daily trash, and recycling removal.
Maintenance: All exterior maintenance, landscaping, and snow removal.
Condo Fees: Covered entirely by the landlord.

Resort-Style Amenities
You'll have full access to incredible community features, including:
3 swimming pools
Tennis, pickleball, and basketball courts
A fully equipped fitness facility/gym
Walking trails and a community party room with a full kitchen

Unmatched Location
Walkable: Just a short stroll to Shirlington's vibrant shops, restaurants, theaters, and the popular Shirlington Dog Park.

Commuter's Dream: Easy access to the Pentagon station, Amazon HQ2, Del Ray, Old Town Alexandria, DCA Airport, and downtown Washington, D.C.
