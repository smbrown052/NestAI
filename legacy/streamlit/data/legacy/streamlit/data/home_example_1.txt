Near Cherry Arms
Grocery shopping in convenient, with a Safeway and a Giant supermarket less than 5 minutes away, and specialty stores like Kang's Market and Ali Baba Groceries are also nearby.
Historic Huntley Meadows is less than a 5-minute drive away and features a restored early 19th-century summer home and boardwalk trails through forests and wetlands.
Shopping is nearby in Beacon Center, which includes a Lowe's Home Improvements, Party Depot, TJMaxx and Carters.
Major employers are just a short drive away, including The National Reconaissance Office and Fort Belvoir, both less than a 15-minute drive away.

Frequently asked questions

Where is Cherry Arms located, and what is the neighborhood like?
Cherry Arms is located at 7131 Richmond Hwy in Hybla Valley, Alexandria, VA. The surrounding area includes approximately 20 restaurants and cafes within 1 mile, such as Starbucks, La Mexicana Taqueria, and Starbucks.


What are the transportation options near Cherry Arms?
The nearest public transit stop is 171 Connector to Huntington, approximately 0 miles away. The nearest major airport is Gone, approximately 0.8 miles away.


What is the average rent in Alexandria, VA?
Based on the latest Zillow data, the average rent across all unit types in Alexandria is currently $3,150 per month. Prices vary by neighborhood, floor plan, and building amenities.


What parks and outdoor spaces are near Cherry Arms?
Cherry Arms is located within 0.3 miles of Meadow Woods, 0.6 miles of Historical Marker Beacon Field Airport, and 0.7 miles of Little Hunting Park.


Are there grocery stores and everyday shopping options near Cherry Arms?
Cherry Arms is located near 3 grocery stores, including 0.5 miles from Fresh World, 0.6 miles from Giant Food, and 0.6 miles from GNC. Additional shopping options, including CVS Pharmacy, are approximately 0.3 miles away.


What is the minimum and maximum rent for Cherry Arms?
The total price for an apartment unit in Cherry Arms ranges from $1,730 to $2,867. The total price will vary by floor plan, unit size, and unit amenities.


How has rent changed recently in Alexandria?
Rent in Alexandria has increased by $350 month-over-month, according to the latest Zillow rental data.


What schools are assigned to Cherry Arms?
The schools assigned to Cherry Arms include Bucknell Elementary School, Sandburg Middle School, and West Potomac High School.
